About Design Me 256

Design Me 256 is a handmade crochet brand based in Kampala, Uganda. We specialize in customized, unique, hand-crafted crochet pieces that combine traditional techniques with a touch of class.

Founded in 2019, we are passionate about delivering high-quality, sophisticated crochet pieces with a modern taste that will add that extra edge to your wardrobe. Whether you are looking for a cozy scarf, a stylish hat, or a custom-made sweater, a chic bag, we have got you covered.

At Design Me 256, every piece is handcrafted with careful attention to detail to ensure you get something truly warm and special.

Our mission is to bring ideas to life through innovative and visually stunning designs that resonate with our clients. Our team consists of skilled designers and creative strategists who will bring your beautiful ideas to life.
