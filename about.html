About Design Me 256

Design Me 256 is a handmade crochet garment cottage industry based in Kampala, Uganda. We specialize in unique, hand-crafted crochet pieces that combine traditional techniques with modern fashion trends.

Founded in 2019, we are passionate about delivering high-quality, bespoke crochet garments that stand out in any wardrobe. Whether you are looking for a cozy scarf, a stylish hat, or a custom-made sweater, we've got you covered.

At Design Me 256, every piece is a labor of love, created with careful attention to detail to ensure you get something truly special.

Our mission is to bring ideas to life through innovative and visually stunning designs that resonate with our clients.

Our team consists of skilled designers and creative strategists who are passionate about delivering results-driven solutions to businesses and individuals worldwide.
